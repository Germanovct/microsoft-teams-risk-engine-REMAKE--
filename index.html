ABOUT US

AGAT is an innovative software provider specializing in security and compliance solutions. AGAT’s award-winning flagship product - SphereShield, is a leading solutionproviding control of data and activities for Unified Communication (UC) & Collaboration services. It enables real time content inspection addressing Data Leak Prevention (DLP), Ethical Wall as well as Anti Malware and eDiscovery requirements.
